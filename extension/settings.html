How Claude Brief works

1

Spot a bug or a thing to fix. Click the ✦ icon, talk through it while showing it on screen, hit stop. The recording saves locally to ~/Downloads/claude-brief/ and lands in your inbox.

2

Capture as many as you like through the day — they queue up in your inbox.

3

Either copy the inbox prompt anytime and paste it into Claude Code Desktop, or let the scheduled daily task do it automatically. Claude reads each brief, files a real ticket in your tracker, and deletes the brief once it's filed.

Everything is local. Briefs never leave your machine until Claude — running on your machine — reads them. Requires Claude Code Desktop with a tracker connected (Linear, GitHub, Jira, Notion).
