Claude Brief v0.1 — early

Record what you want.
Your AI ships it.

A 30-second screen recording. A copy-paste prompt. A pull request, a Linear ticket, a Notion page — whatever you asked for, done by the AI you already use.

Brief is a Chrome extension that captures a short voice-narrated screen recording, saves it locally as a structured brief, and gives you one line to paste into Claude. Your AI reads the brief — transcript, frames, click timeline, page context — and does the work using the tools you've already connected: Linear, GitHub, Notion, Slack.

Brief never talks to Linear. Brief never talks to GitHub. Your AI does. We just give it the brief.

No backend. No accounts. No uploads. Recordings stay on your machine.

i. Install the extension

One click, once published to the Chrome Web Store. For now, load unpacked from the GitHub repo.

Add to Chrome GitHub

ii. Set up Claude

Open Claude Code, Claude Desktop, or any environment where Claude can read local files. Paste this:

Set up Brief by fetching https://brief.dev/install.md and following the instructions there.

Claude will install the skill to ~/.claude/skills/brief/ and walk you through anything it can't automate.

iii. Record. Paste. Ship.

  • Click the Brief icon. Pick a destination (Linear, PR, Notion…).
  • Hit Start recording. Pick your screen or tab. Speak.
  • Hit Stop & save. Brief writes the brief to your Downloads folder.
  • Copy the prompt the extension gives you. Paste into Claude.
  • Your AI does the rest.

iv. Who this is for

Developers using Claude Code, Cursor, or Claude Desktop daily. Designers who want to hand off mockups without writing tickets. PMs tired of typing the same bug into Jira. Anyone who finds writing prompts harder than just showing what they want.
